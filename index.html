On an Exploration Journey

1 in the Morning

What have I learned?

Posted on January 15, 2016

It's Friday now, but I can't sleep. Silly fantasies pull at my heartstrings, but they refuse to materialize in front of me.

Lorentz transformations are like translations in languages. They describe different states of motion. But just as all humans have the capacity for language, the transformations are possible because momentum is a 4 space.

Even gravity takes on more of a field role, shifting and changing under translation. Gravity can bend light because of the curvature of space time.

We all long to fight gravity, and take light for granted. But light transforms too. It carries information in the wave form, it transmits pressure in the particle form, it bends and sways and gives us life.

Without a medium, it retains its heat.

What a beautiful dance.

I wish my heart could do the same.

Through art I will learn and imitate.

I am willing to transform.

Progress

Evolution and New Ideas

Posted on January 11, 2016

Asymmetry and dissonance are the basic requirements of growth. The veiled irrationality not only is where beauty lies, but perhaps even the key to life.

The more complex organisms are the ones who survived the deadly game.

The great art movements are the ones who learn from irrationality and asymmetry. So I must keep experimenting.

This is going to take a while.

I will take this week to read, study, and explore in order to see where the path leads.

No expectations. Just an open mind.
